# Necrobinder Decision Guide for Current STS2 Data

This guide is based on `runtime/reference-library.json`. Necrobinder has no STS1 baseline, so the recommendations here are entirely about the current STS2 pool: Doom, Souls, Osty, Ethereal cards, and summon management.

## Default Draft Plan

If no build is open yet, draft in this order:

1. Reliable Doom application or reliable Osty pressure.
2. Card flow.
3. One clear scaling package.
4. Defensive cards that stay relevant.
5. Removes and transforms.

In practice, that means early rewards should usually prefer cards like `Negative Pulse`, `Scourge`, `Grave Warden`, `Parse`, `Dredge`, `Fear`, `Putrefy`, `Bodyguard`, `Invoke`, and `Pull Aggro` over slow payoff cards with no shell.

## Best Packages

### 1. Doom Kill

This is the cleanest late-game plan in the class.

Core enablers:

- `Negative Pulse`
- `Scourge`
- `Blight Strike`
- `No Escape`
- `Countdown`

Best payoffs:

- `Time's Up`
- `End of Days`
- `Shroud`
- `Undying Sigil`
- `Book Repair Knife`
- `Deathbringer`
- `Reaper Form`

Selection rule:

- `End of Days` and `Time's Up` are payoffs, not setup. Do not draft them before the deck can stack Doom consistently.
- `Undying Sigil` is premium when this lane is open because it stabilizes the dangerous turns before Doom cashes out.
- `Book Repair Knife` is a major survivability boost in real Doom decks.

### 2. Souls Engine

This is the best pure value shell when the deck wants to cycle and scale at the same time.

Core enablers:

- `Capture Spirit`
- `Grave Warden`
- `Reave`
- `Severance`
- `Funerary Mask`

Best payoffs:

- `Soul Storm`
- `Devour Life`
- `Haunt`
- `Glimpse Beyond`
- `Seance`

Selection rule:

- `Soul Storm` is mediocre early and excellent once Souls are entering and exhausting repeatedly.
- `Devour Life` and `Haunt` turn Souls from draw smoothing into real scaling.
- `Funerary Mask` is one of the class's best relics because it starts this engine every combat for free.

### 3. Osty Attack/Summon

This is the most direct tempo plan in the class.

Core enablers:

- `Bodyguard`
- `Afterlife`
- `Invoke`
- `Pull Aggro`
- `Spur`

Best payoffs:

- `Calcify`
- `Protector`
- `Unleash`
- `Rattle`
- `Sic 'Em`
- `Squeeze`
- `Bone Flute`

Selection rule:

- Do not draft too many Osty attacks without enough `Summon`.
- `Bound Phylactery` is a huge stabilizer because it quietly keeps the summon total moving every turn.
- `Bone Flute` turns normal Osty turns into real block scaling.

### 4. Ethereal Tempo

This is the most STS2-feeling side package in the class.

Core enablers:

- `Sculpting Strike`
- `Call of the Void`
- `Pagestorm`
- `Veilpiercer`
- `Big Hat`

Best payoffs:

- `Pull from Below`
- `Spirit of Ash`
- `Banshee's Cry`
- `Eidolon`

Selection rule:

- Do not force this line without enough repeatable Ethereal generation.
- `Big Hat` is much better when the deck already rewards drawing or playing Ethereal cards.

## Remove, Transform, and Upgrade Priorities

### Removes

Default rule:

- Remove `Strike` first.

Exceptions:

- In a pure Doom-control deck, mediocre direct attacks become worse than `Defend` very quickly.
- Remove curses before basics unless the deck can reliably exhaust or ignore them.

### Transforms

Default rule:

- Transform `Strike` first.

Exceptions:

- In a mature Osty deck with strong summon support, dead non-Osty attacks can be better transform targets than `Defend`.

### Best Upgrades

Highest impact:

- `End of Days`
- `Devour Life`
- `Countdown`
- `Deathbringer`
- `Reanimate`
- `Protector`
- `No Escape`
- `Parse`

Very strong when relevant:

- `Negative Pulse`
- `Scourge`
- `Grave Warden`
- `Invoke`
- `Putrefy`
- `Soul Storm`
- `Squeeze`

Low-value upgrades unless they fix a breakpoint:

- `Strike`
- `Defend`
- `Wisp`
- `Defile`

## Necrobinder Relic Priorities

Top class relics:

- `Bound Phylactery`
- `Funerary Mask`
- `Undying Sigil`

Very strong and frequently enabling:

- `Book Repair Knife`
- `Bone Flute`
- `Bookmark`
- `Big Hat`

Notes:

- `Bound Phylactery` is one of the strongest starter relics in the game because it keeps almost every Necrobinder shell online.
- `Bookmark` gets much better if the deck has premium retained cards or repeatedly creates retained cards.
- `Ivory Tile` is strong only when your curve genuinely supports it.

## Event Choices That Matter Most

### Usually Excellent

- `Dense Vegetation`: remove a card for `11 HP` if your life total is stable. Necrobinder scales much better with cleaner draws.
- `Doors of Light and Dark`: remove `1` over random upgrading `2` unless the deck is already trimmed and full of upgrade targets.
- `Morphic Grove`: transform `2` for `100 gold` when basics are still clogging setup.
- `Whispering Hollow`: transforming a card for `9 HP` is usually better than two random potions.
- `Zen Weaver`: premium remove event.

### Strong With Necrobinder-Specific Context

- `Luminous Choir`: `remove 2 + Spore Mind` is much more acceptable here than for most classes if the deck already likes Souls, exhaust, or other disposable cards.
- `Grave of the Forgotten`: both lines can be good. `Forgotten Soul` is the safer pick; the `Decay + Soul's Power` line is strongest when you already have premium exhaust targets.
- `Hungry for Mushrooms`: `Fragrant Mushroom` is usually better than `Big Mushroom`; Necrobinder values clean opening turns and does not want to start every combat down two draws.
- `Tea Master`: temporary opening-hand upgrades are strong when your deck needs to land Doom or summon setup immediately.
- `This or That?`: the cursed relic line is more tolerable here than for many classes if your deck can exhaust or outvalue dead cards, but it is still not the default.

### Usually Avoid

- `Field of Man-Sized Holes`: avoid `Normality`. Necrobinder often needs multi-card setup turns.
- `Wellspring`: `Guilty` is usually too much cost for one remove unless the deck is already very stable.

## Drafting Rules That Hold Up

- Do not split focus between Doom, Souls, and Osty offense without a reason. Pick a primary engine.
- Doom needs setup first and payoff second.
- The class becomes much stronger once it stops drawing irrelevant basics, so removes are especially valuable.
- If a deterministic remove or transform is competing against an enchant line with unclear output, prefer the deterministic line.
