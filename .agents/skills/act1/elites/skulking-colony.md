# Skulking Colony

## What It Is

- Act 1 elite
- Closest useful mental model:
  - capped-damage threshold fight

## Current Read

- This is not a free early elite even when the numbers look modest.
- `Hardened Shell` changes the whole fight: once the deck can hit the per-turn damage cap, extra raw attack text stops mattering and block efficiency matters more.
- The fight gets much cleaner when the deck can combine efficient `Vulnerable`, one or two strong attacks, and enough block to avoid leaking while still hitting the cap.
- Relic text that adds damage without spending cards, like `Parrying Shield`, is especially valuable here because it helps finish capped turns while preserving hand quality.

## Practical Rule

- Do not ask only whether the deck has frontload. Ask whether it can hit the cap and still defend.
- Shop-bought `Vulnerable` support can materially change this elite from sketchy to earned.
- If the deck cannot reliably hit capped turns, treat the fight as much scarier than its printed numbers suggest.

## Failure Mode To Avoid

- overcommitting attacks after the cap is already effectively reached
- taking the elite with raw damage but no way to preserve HP through the non-cap turns
- treating the shop as optional value instead of the node that earns the elite

## Durable Lesson

- The important lesson from the known winning attempts is still route permission, but now with a clearer rule: `Skulking Colony` is earned when the deck can convert each turn into capped damage efficiently, not merely when it has a pile of attacks.
