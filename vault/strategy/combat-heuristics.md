# Combat Heuristics

General fight rules distilled from live STS2 runs.

## Core Rule

- Do not judge a line only by current-turn efficiency.
- Judge it by whether it keeps the next cycle winnable.

## Setup Threshold

- Learn to identify `I am fucked if I do not set up`.
- In those states, setup is mandatory, not greedy.
- This matters most in elites, bosses, and scaling fights.

## Tempo Vs Position

- Spending all energy is not the goal.
- Building a winning position is the goal.
- A lower-tempo setup turn can be stronger than a full-energy damage turn.

## Potion Timing

- Some potions are strongest on turn 1 when the opener is weak.
- Use a potion when it changes the shape of the fight: block breakpoint, lethal breakpoint, or setup breakpoint.
- Do not spend a potion just because the turn is mediocre.

## Threat Priority

- Kill or weaken the source of future damage, status clutter, or scaling.
- If one enemy attacks and one enemy adds junk, decide which future is more dangerous, not just which HP bar is lower.

## Resource Discipline

- HP is a resource, but not a dump stat.
- Accept damage when it buys a materially stronger next cycle.
- Do not pay HP for lines that are only marginally better than the safer alternative.

## Status And Junk

- Treat draw pile pollution as future damage.
- Fights that add junk punish slow, attack-heavy lines more than they first appear to.
